7. Children’s Privacy

fairydust offers a limited-access “Kid Mode” for children under 13, which must be enabled by a parent or guardian.

Kid Mode does not allow content sharing, personal messaging, or data collection from the child. It limits access to select features such as creative games and storytelling tools. A PIN is required to exit Kid Mode.

We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. Optional child profile information (first name, birthday, photo) can be entered by the adult user and is used only to personalize the Kid Mode experience within that account.

If we become aware of unintended data collection from a child, we will delete it promptly. Parents or guardians may request review or deletion of any child-related data at any time by contacting us.
